AI-Driven Public Health Chatbot

Empowering Disease Awareness Through Artificial Intelligence

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve public health awareness by providing accurate, accessible, and AI-powered disease information that helps people understand symptoms, prevention, and healthy practices.

What We Do

The AI-Driven Public Health Chatbot is an intelligent assistant designed to educate users about diseases, reduce misinformation, and support healthier communities through reliable health information.

Key Features

  • AI-powered health chatbot
  • Disease symptoms, causes, and prevention
  • Simple and easy-to-understand language
  • 24/7 availability
  • Privacy-focused and secure

Diseases Covered

  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
  • Dengue
  • Malaria
  • COVID-19
  • Anemia
  • Respiratory Infections

Target Audience

This chatbot is designed for the general public, students, rural and urban communities, NGOs, and individuals seeking basic health awareness.

Primary Goals

  • Education through verified health information
  • Trust-building using reliable sources
  • User engagement through interactive chat

Interactive Elements

  • Chat-based question and answer interface
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
  • Health awareness statistics
  • User feedback form

Technology Used

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Cloud-based infrastructure
  • Secure data handling

Our Vision

To create a healthy and informed society where technology bridges the gap between healthcare information and people.
